Help & Support

We're here to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have. Please check the FAQs below or feel free to contact us for further support.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are your store hours?

We are open from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM Monday through Saturday, and from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM on Sundays.

2. How do I book a grooming appointment?

You can book a grooming appointment by visiting our Grooming page and filling out the appointment form, or by giving us a call at (123) 456-7890.

3. Do you offer delivery for products?

Yes, we offer delivery for products purchased online. Please visit our Products page for more information and to place an order.

4. What pets do you currently have available for adoption?

We have a variety of pets ready for adoption. Please visit our Pets page to see the current list of animals available and learn more about them.

5. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email at support@petshop.com or by phone at (123) 456-7890. We are happy to help!
